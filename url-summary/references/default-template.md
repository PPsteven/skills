# Default Article Template

Use this template for general web content: blog posts, news articles, opinion pieces, tutorials, and general informational content.

## Structure

```markdown
---

author: <author name or "Unknown">
date: <publication date or fetch date>
source: <original URL>
tags: [<relevant>, <topic>, <tags>]
created: <YYYY-MM-DD>
---
```

## Guidelines

1. **Summary length**: Aim for 150-300 words in the summary section
2. **Key points**: Extract 3-7 key points, prioritize the most important
3. **Detailed notes**: Only include sections that contain significant information
4. **Quotes**: Include 2-4 memorable or impactful quotes if available
5. **Takeaways**: Focus on actionable insights or lessons learned
6. **Tags**: Use 3-5 relevant tags for organization

## What to Include

- Main thesis or argument
- Supporting evidence and examples
- Author's perspective and opinions
- Practical applications or advice
- Interesting data or statistics
- Relevant context or background

## What to Omit

- Redundant information
- Excessive marketing language
- Unrelated tangents
- Overly detailed descriptions of obvious points
